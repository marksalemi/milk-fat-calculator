Milk Fat Calculator

Do you need a milk/cream with a fat percentage you don't have? You can use this calculator to mix different milk/creams to get a desired milk fat percentage. Example do you need Half and Half but only have 2% and whipping cream, this calculator will give you the amounts of each to mix together to get Half and Half.
